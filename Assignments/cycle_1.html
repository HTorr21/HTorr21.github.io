Canvas

To create puzzle I first used the rectangle tool to create a grid like pattern of squares on one layer. On another layer I fit my image, which I rasterized, onto this grid layout. After locking the image back in place I went back to the first layer and using the ellipse tool added small circles to the flat parts of the squares that touched on another. Next I utilized pathfinder and the unite function to join the two shapes creating a puzzle piece shape.

Inorder to make sure that the peices are cut through using vectors cuts I made sure that the lines were 0.01 mm. I am not yet able to expand on the lazer cutting process given the current condition of the lazer cutter.

I do not yet have the final product so I am unable to speak more about how lazer cutting my puzzle went. What I do know is that I have to make sure that I adjust the speed as to not start fires and to constantly monitor the machine while it is in use.
